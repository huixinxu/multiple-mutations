Welcome to SOME: the Significance of Mutation Engine

What is SOME?

SOME is a tool to determine the statistical significance of multiple mutations found in (almost) any gene.
It allows you to upload your data in the format indicated below and returns a list of the genes with multiple mutations, and the significance of those mutations.

(Please be sure that your data file contains only one gene and one of its associated mutations per line)
